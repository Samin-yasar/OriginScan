2. Description of Services

In Short: OriginScan provides barcode scanning to identify product origins, with features like scan history and reporting.

OriginScan is a client-side barcode scanning tool that decodes barcodes (e.g., EAN-13, UPC-A) via camera, image uploads, or manual input to provide information about product origins. Features include displaying results in the "Product Origin" section, storing scans in the "Scan History," aggregating scan counts in "Scans Today," and allowing suspicious barcode reports. The Services rely on third-party APIs (e.g., OpenStreetMap, Rest Countries) for data such as country coordinates and population. All scanning and data processing occur locally in your browser, except for suspicious barcode reports, which are sent to our server.
